
# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into customer spending patterns, product performance, and revenue distribution across different dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand customer purchase behavior
- Identify top and bottom performing products
- Analyze revenue trends across locations and categories
- Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Metrics:
  - Total Customers
  - Total Spend
  - Average Spend
  - Average Rating
  - Unique Items

- Interactive Filters:
  - Category
  - Gender
  - Discount Applied
  - Subscription Status

- Visual Insights:
  - Top & Bottom 5 Products by Ratings
  - Top & Bottom 5 Products by Revenue
  - Revenue by Category, Gender, Season
  - Revenue by Payment Method
  - Shipping Type Analysis
  - Location-wise Revenue Analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Electronics category generates the highest revenue
- Major cities like New York and Los Angeles contribute significantly
- Discounts influence customer purchasing behavior
- Seasonal trends show higher sales in Summer and Winter
- Cash and card payments dominate revenue share
